Our Story

বাংলা এখানে।
Bengal is here.

It started with homesickness.

When Arpan moved from Kolkata to Bangalore, the city offered everything — opportunity, energy, warmth. But there was one thing no restaurant could give: the taste of home.

Bengalis in Bangalore know the feeling well. You crave sorshe mach on a rainy afternoon. You miss the Sunday kosha mangsho that would slow-cook for hours while you read the newspaper. You want the simple comfort of luchi-chholar dal on a lazy morning.

Bangla Ekhane was born from that longing — and from one simple belief: great Bengali food doesn't need to be fancy. It needs to be honest. Made with the right mustard oil, the right ratio of spices, and the patience that only comes from truly caring.

So here we are. In the heart of Bangalore, cooking the way our mothers and grandmothers taught us. With love, without shortcuts.
